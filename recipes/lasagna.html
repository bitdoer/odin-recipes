Lasagna

Description

A layered dish consisting of pasta, meat, and cheese. Time-consuming, but more than worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat (sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef)
  • An onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato juice
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest before serving
