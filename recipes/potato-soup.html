Potato Soup

Description

A hearty, filling, and savory soup for those freezing winter nights by the fire.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Baked potatoes
  • Green onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Melt the butter, then whisk in flour and milk
  3. Add potatoes and onions and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce to a simmer, then stir in remaining ingredients
  5. Cook until cheese is melted
